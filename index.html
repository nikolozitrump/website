About

A Georgian evening, hidden in Vera.

Tucked into a quiet courtyard off Mikheil Zandukeli Street, Giorgobistve brings together simple Georgian food, a green garden and a calm indoor dining room ‚Äì a spot for long dinners with friends, family gatherings or a glass of wine after work.

What you'll find here

The kitchen focuses on comforting, regional Georgian recipes ‚Äì grilled meats straight from the mangal, cheese breads, seasonal salads and warm dishes made to share.

  • Leafy courtyard with veranda-style seating in the shade
  • Indoor dining room for cooler evenings and winter nights
  • Selection of local wines and classic Georgian spirits
  • Plates designed for sharing ‚Äì from light snacks to full feasts
